# Consult Hub - User Manual

## Getting started

Open the web portal (or mobile app) and sign in. You'll be routed to the area for your role.

## For Clients

1. **Register / Sign in** - create an account or log in.
2. **Browse consultants** (Find tab) - view consultant profiles, start a chat or book a meeting.
3. **Buy a package** (Packages) - choose a one-time consultation or mentorship plan, optionally pick a consultant, then complete payment via PayFast. Your allowances activate automatically once payment is confirmed.
4. **Message** (Messages) - send text, audio or video messages. Your remaining allowances are shown on your dashboard.
5. **Book meetings** (Meetings) - request a session in a consultant's available slot; you'll be notified when it's approved.
6. **Communities** - join free or paid groups, post and comment.
7. **Payments** - review your invoices and payment history.

## For Consultants

1. **Dashboard** - see your clients and their package utilization.
2. **Clients** - view clients assigned to you.
3. **Messages** - respond to client text/audio/video messages.
4. **Meetings** - approve, reject, reschedule or complete meeting requests.
5. **Availability** - set your weekly available hours for bookings.
6. **Communities & Notifications** - participate in groups and stay updated.

## For Admins

1. **Dashboard** - KPIs across clients, consultants, revenue, meetings and messages.
2. **Consultants / Clients** - create accounts, activate/deactivate.
3. **Packages** - create consultation & mentorship packages; set every limit (messages, sessions, durations). Leave a limit blank for unlimited.
4. **Communities** - create free or paid communities.
5. **Payments** - view all transactions and invoices.
6. **Settings** - configure platform name, currency, durations, branding and more.

## Notes
- A blank limit on any package field means **unlimited**.
- When PayFast credentials are not configured, the platform uses a mock checkout so you can test the full flow.
- Emails are logged to the server console until SMTP is configured.
